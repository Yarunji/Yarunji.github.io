Misa Amane

Misa Amane appears cheerful, emotional, and impulsive—someone who acts on intuition rather than logic. Her devotion to Light Yagami is intense,
bordering on dependence, which makes her motivations easy to understand but her actions difficult to predict.
Though she presents herself as careless and playful, Misa is not without cunning. She can be observant and manipulative when her emotions drive her,
but she lacks the restraint and strategic thinking to fully conceal her intentions. Her decisions are guided by passion, not reason.
Given the nature of the Second Kira’s messages—dramatic, attention-seeking, and emotionally charged—Misa fits the psychological profile closely.
Her desire for recognition and her unwavering loyalty to those she idolizes make her a highly plausible suspect.
In essence, Misa is a volatile blend of devotion, impulsiveness, and emotional intensity—traits that align disturbingly well with the behaviors of the Second Kira.
