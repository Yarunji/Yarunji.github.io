Teru Mikami

Teru Mikami is a man defined by rigid conviction. His worldview is absolute—there are only those who deserve salvation and those who deserve judgment,
with no space for ambiguity in between. This unwavering belief system makes him both predictable and extremely dangerous.
Mikami’s strict discipline and almost ascetic lifestyle indicate a deep need for order.
He follows rules with religious intensity, yet paradoxically, he submits himself entirely to a higher authority once he deems it “just”.
This devotion is not based on rational evaluation but on blind faith. He is intelligent, meticulous, and unyieldingly loyal to the ideals he reveres. However,
that loyalty is paired with a troubling lack of independent moral reasoning. Mikami does not question—he obeys, and he believes his obedience is righteous.
In essence, Teru Mikami is a zealot: unwavering, disciplined, and utterly convinced of the correctness of his path.
Such a person, when aligned with Kira, becomes a perfect instrument—and a profound threat.
